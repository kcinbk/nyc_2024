A very Detailed Map of the 2024 Presidential Election Results in New York City

By Keenan Chen

President-elect Donald Trump gained substantially more support in New York City in the 2024 general election. Compared to his 2020 race, he improved by adding more than 94,000 votes in over 75 percent of the 4000-plus voting precincts across the five boroughs.

These two interactive maps illustrate Trump's increase in vote share and total number of votes across. Try search for an address, zip code, or neighborhood name in the search bar at the upper right of the map, or hover over the map to view detailed data for a voting precinct.

The precinct-level data, including the official vote count from the 2020 general election and the unofficial 2024 count, is New York City's Board of Elections.
