A Very Detailed Map of the 2024 Presidential Election Result in New York City

By Keenan Chen

President-elect Donald Trump gained substantially more support in New York City in the 2024 general election. Compared to his 2020 race, he improved by adding more than 94,000 votes in over 75 percent of the 4000-plus voting precincts across the five boroughs.

To look up precinct-level result, try hover over a specicific area on the map, or type in an address, a zip code, or a neighborhood name in the search bar located on the upper right of the map.

Data from New York City's Board of Elections and US Census Bureau
