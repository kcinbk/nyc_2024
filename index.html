A very detailed map on Trump's voter-share gain in New York City

This interactive map illustrates how Donald Trump has gained support across Queens, including Whitestone, Forest Park and Ozone Park, as well as in Brooklyn, such as Crown Heights, Williamsburg, and Borough Park -- neigborhoods that are mostly represented by Democratic elected officials in the City Hall and in Albany.

The source of this precinct-level data, including the official vote count from the 2020 general election and the unofficial count from 2024, is New York City's Board of Elections.
