A very detailed map on Trump's voter-share gain in New York City

By Keenan Chen

Try searching for an address, zip code, or neighborhood name in the search bar at the upper right of the map, or hover over the map to view detailed data for a voting precinct.

The source of this precinct-level data, including the official vote count from the 2020 general election and the unofficial count from 2024, is New York City's Board of Elections.
